Privacy Policy – GPS Tagger

Last updated: 12 January 2026

GPS Tagger ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting user data. This Privacy Policy explains how the GPS Tagger Android application collects, uses, and safeguards information in compliance with Google Play Data Safety requirements.

1. Data Collection Overview

GPS Tagger collects only limited, user-initiated data required for core functionality. We do not sell, share, or store personal data on external servers.

2. Data Types Collected

2.1 Photos and Media

  • Photos are captured only when the user taps the capture button.
  • All images are stored locally on the user's device.
  • No images are uploaded or transmitted to any server.

2.2 Location Data

  • Precise location is accessed only during image capture.
  • Used to display live location and accurate address on the image.
  • Location data is not tracked, stored, or shared externally.

2.3 Advertising Data

GPS Tagger uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and basic usage data in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

3. Data Usage

Data is used only for:

  • Capturing photos
  • Displaying live location and address
  • Allowing image sharing and deletion
  • Displaying advertisements

Data is not used for profiling, analytics tracking, or background location access.

4. Data Sharing

GPS Tagger does not share user data with third parties. Only Google AdMob independently processes advertising-related data.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All data remains on the user’s device.
  • No cloud storage or backend servers are used.
  • Deleted images are permanently removed.

6. User Control

  • Users can share images manually using system share options.
  • Users can delete images at any time.
  • Permissions can be revoked from device settings.

7. Permissions Used

android.permission.CAMERA
android.permission.ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION
android.permission.READ_MEDIA_IMAGES (Android 13+)
android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 10–12)
android.permission.INTERNET

All permissions are requested only when required for app functionality.

8. Children’s Privacy

GPS Tagger does not target children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected by updating the revision date on this page.
